Swinging at the end of the world

I am getting even more excited for my South America tour in Spring 2018! I just booked a bus ride to the swing at the end of the world with my wife and one of my friends. My wife prefers just to watch and capture this moment because she is afraid of heights.

I will visit Ecuador in the course of my South America tour in March. The swing has been on my mental bucket list for long, because I am ready to challenge myself and I heard that the view on the Ecuadorian rainforest from the swing must be beautiful.
