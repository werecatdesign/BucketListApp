Sledding down a volcano

Wait, what? Have you lost your mind, you might ask. That was exactly the same question I asked to my son who suggested to do a trip to Indonesia together and... sled down a volcano.

We've been planning this trip for a while now, and my son is very adventurous. Eventually, I agreed to do this because you only live once and I want to find back to my adventurous spirit from the past days.

My son and I are going to travel to Java in early September 2017, if you would like to join in, please contact me.
