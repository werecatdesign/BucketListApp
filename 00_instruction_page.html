INSTRUCTIONS




The Bucket List App offers pensioners a digital platform to plan, organize and share their lifetime goals. In order to reach their goals more capably, you can connect and collaborate with other users and optionally participate in a monthly lottery.


If you need help with the content of the page and the functionality of the buttons, click on the question mark button in the top right corner of the screen and read the given information.


When you read the given information you can always exit the information window by clicking on the "X" button on the upper right corner.


On the home page of the app, you can access your bucket list, your profile, your contacts, the discovery page and the lottery page, by clicking on the buttons.


On the 'Bucket list' page you can see all the items you have on your bucket list. If you want to add another item, you can click on the button shown on the picture and add a goal to complete.


When you want to add a new item to your bucket list, a window with a texting box is displayed, and you can write your goal there.When you are ready you can click 'Save', to save the item to your bucket list, or 'Cancel' to undo the action.


If you want to read more about an item on the bucket list you can click on it and you will have access to the detailed information about the item.


If you want to go back to the page with the items on the bucket list, you have to click on the back button in th lower left corner of the screen. Every time when you want to go back to the previous pages, you can use this button


On the 'Bucket list' page you can also access the 'Experiences' page where you can see all the items from the bucket list that have been completed.


If you want to see detailed information about the experiences that you or the other users of the app that have been completed, you need to click on the picture, and the details will be shown.


Whenever you want to go the 'Home page', you have to click on the home button in the upper left corner of the screen.


If you want to review your personal information, you can click on "About me" on your profile page.


From your Profile page, you can also access the messages page. As well as your Bucket list and your experiences.


In the messages page, you can review all the messages you have received from the other users of the app. You can open the chat by clicking on the message.


If you want to send a message to someone from your contacts, you just have to write your message in the text box, and click on 'SEND'.


On the 'Contacts' page you can open the profile of someone of the other users, by clicking on the profile picture. You have also the option to send them a message or to delete the contact from your contact list.


When you are in someone else's bucket list, you can add some of their bucket list items to your bucket list, by clicking on the '+' that is next to the item. You can also send a message to that user, by clicking the message button in the lower right corner of the screen.


If you want to get an inspiration you can go to 'Discover' page and choose any of the categories, and review the other users' bucket lists.


If you like something and you want to add it to your bucket list just click on the button '+', and the item will be added to your list. You also have the option to see the other users' bucket lists or to send them a message.


You can participate in the monthly lottery of our app.


If you want to participate in the lottery you need to become a premium user of the Bucket List App.


If you want to participate in the lottery you need to become a premium user of the Bucket List App. When you are premium user you have the chance to win up to €100 for the next endeavor on your bucket list!


You can disable your premium membership any time from your profile page.






Thank you for downloading the Bucket List app! We hope you will have fun while using it.
