Backpack adventure in Scotland

I want to enjoy every moment of my life.I am a true explorer, an adventurous spirit and young at heart!

I am going to organize a backpacking adventure in Scotland. Stac Pollaidh is one of the best ‘little mountains’ in Scotland. Standing at just 613m high in the Northwest Highlands, have you heard of it? I can't wait to see the peak that displays a rocky crest of the Torridonian sandstone. It is going to be so much fun.
